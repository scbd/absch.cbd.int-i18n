Send Checkpoint Communiqué

Since you have already been setup National Authorized User(NAU) for your country.You sign-in to the ABSCH and fill out the Checkpoint Communiqué.

You are lucky.The User has provided you with the IRCC which makes filling out the Checkpoint Communique much more simple.

The User has provided you with the national permit.It will take a little longer to fill out the CPC form.You enter all the information possible including the national permit identifier.

The User has only provided you with the name of country where he got the magic beans.It will take a little longer to fill out the CPC form.

After you fill out the form.You request it to be published by the Publishing Authority(PA).

Once published by the PA the ABSCH will email a copy to the designated national authority/ies within their own country and to the national focal point of the country and the competent national authority/ies(CNA) of the country providing the genetic resource.
