ABSCH for developers (api.cbd.int)

The ABS Clearing-House has been designed to be interoperable and facilitate the exchange of information with other databases and systems, in particular Parties’ databases, as well as databases of other instruments and organizations

С этой целью Секретариат разработал интерфейс программных приложений (ИПП). The API is the main tool to facilitate interoperability and the exchange of information with other IT systems or databases, in particular the IT systems of Parties. ИПП обеспечивает стандартный интерфейс, который описывается набором стандартных протоколов и облегчает взаимодействие с компьютерными системами. The API is meant to allow the seamless integration of the data and functionality of the ABSCH, in order to avoid duplication, into other systems in ways that best fit their own needs, workflows and technologies.
