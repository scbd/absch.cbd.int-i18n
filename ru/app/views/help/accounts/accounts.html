Как зарегистрировать учетную запись КБР?

Любое лицо может иметь учетную запись КБР, обеспечивающую доступ к функциям регистрации информации во всех механизмах посредничества КБР, включая Механизм посредничества по биобезопасности (МПБ), Механизм посредничества для регулирования ДГРСИВ (МП-ДГРСИВ) и механизм посредничества (МП) в рамках Конвенции. Зарегистрировать учетную запись КБР можно по ссылке: accounts.cbd.int/signin

Удостоверьтесь в том, что вы предоставили всю требуемую информацию в соответствии с приведенными в форме инструкциями. Вы получите по электронной почте автоматическое сообщение со ссылкой, по которой следует перейти, чтобы подтвердить свою учетную запись.

 I am not sure if my account is still active?

The easiest way to check if you are registered accounts.cbd.int is to go to the forgot password page and enter the email associated with that account. If you have an account you will be emailed by the system a link to re-activate your password.

Do I need a CBD account for searching information in the ABS-CH?

No. You can search information and consult the country profiles without requiring a CBD account.

What kind of information can I register in the ABS-CH having a CBD account?

The type of information you can register will depend on your role in the ABS-CH. National records can only be registered by national authorized users (NAUs) or publishing authority (PA) duly designated by a country.

However, any registered user will be able to register Reference Records in the Virtual Library.

Забыли пароль?

You can click here accounts.cbd.int/password/reset. You will be asked for your email address and, if you are a registered user, an email will be sent immediately to your registered email account with instructions to reset your password.

Problems signing in? Check your internet browser.

At the moment, the ABS-CH works best with the most recent verisons of internet browsers (Firefox, Chrome or Internet Explorer), and sometimes problems arise from having older versions of the browsers. We will continue working to improve browser compatibility, but in the meanwhile, please make sure you have the most recent version of your favorite browser installed on your computer:

  • For Firefox: http://www.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/new /
  • For Chrome: https://www.google.com/intl/en/chrome/browser/ei
  • For Internet explorer: http://windows.microsoft.com/en-ca/internet-explorer/download-ie
If your problems persists please contact us at absch@cbd.int

How do I update my profile?

You can update your profile here: accounts.cbd.int/profile. You will be asked to sign-in before you can update your profile information.

Please note, to set up your account as a Publishing Authority (PA) or National Authorized User (NAU) will need to be officially deisgnated by the ABS or National Focal Point.
