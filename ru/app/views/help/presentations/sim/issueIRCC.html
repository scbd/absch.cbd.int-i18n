Issue a national permit and IRCC

You issue a national permit and file the information into your national system. You sign-in to the ABSCH and then register and publish information on the permit issued with a view to have an internationally recognized certificate of compliance. Remember that the user asked you to keep the genetic resource confidential. The information registered gets published in the ABSCH and an internationally recognized certificate of compliance (IRCC) is constituted from the information submitted about the permit. The IRCC contains a unique identifier (UID).

The ABSCH sends a copy of the IRCC to you, to the National Focal Point of your country and to the User.
