Registering National Records

The submission of records in this category is restricted to Biosafety Clearing-House National Focal Points (BCH-NFPs) and National Authorized Users (NAUs). All records submitted by NAUs will be forwarded to the BCH-NFP for review and validation prior to publication (see FAQs).
