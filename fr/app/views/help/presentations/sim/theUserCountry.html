The User Country: Wizards Kingdom

In this story you will be asked to make decisions on behalf of the user's country. The user is from a far away place called Wizards Kingdom. Wizard’s Kingdom has ratified the Nagoya Protocol.

The User Country's goal is:
  1. to comply with the Nagoya Protocol;
  2. to take measures to monitor the utilization of genetic resources, including the designation of one or more effective checkpoints;
  3. to take measures to provide that magic genetic resources and associated magic knowledge utilized within Wizards Kingdom have been accessed with prior informed consent (PIC) and mutually agreed terms (MAT) as required by the ABS measures of the country providing such resources or knowledge.
